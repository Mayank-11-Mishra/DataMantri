## ğŸ¤ Voiceover Guidelines

### **Tone & Style:**
- **Professional but friendly**
- **Confident and clear**
- **Not too fast, not too slow** (140-160 words per minute)
- **Enthusiastic about features** without being salesy
- **Gender:** Male or female (consistent across both videos)
- **Accent:** Neutral (American/British English preferred)

### **Recording Tips:**
1. **Use a pop filter** to reduce plosives (P, B sounds)
2. **Record in a quiet room** with minimal echo
3. **Stay consistent** with distance from microphone
4. **Breath control** - edit out breathing sounds
5. **Record extra takes** of difficult sections
6. **Add pauses** where visuals need more time

### **Script Timing:**
- **AI Dashboard:** ~300-350 words = 2-3 minutes
- **Data Management Suite:** ~450-500 words = 3-4 minutes

## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### **Recording:**
1. **Record at 60fps** even if exporting at 30fps (smoother slow-mo)
2. **Use a second monitor** to view script while recording
3. **Record extra B-roll** of UI elements for transitions
4. **Save often** and in multiple locations
5. **Use keyboard shortcuts** for smoother navigation

### **Editing:**
1. **Create a template** with intro/outro animations for consistency
2. **Use adjustment layers** for color grading (easier to tweak)
3. **Nest sequences** for complex animations
4. **Export intermediate versions** to save progress
5. **Watch on different screens** to check quality

### **Voiceover:**
1. **Script on paper** is easier to read than on screen
2. **Stand up** for better breath support
3. **Smile while speaking** for a more friendly tone
4. **Record in morning** when voice is fresh
5. **Hydrate** but avoid dairy before recording
